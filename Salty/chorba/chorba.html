CHORBA FRIK

Description

Chorba Hamra bel Frik (Algerian Lamb, Tomato, and Freekeh Soup)

Chorba frik is a soup frequently prepared during the month of Ramadan. It is usually accompanied by briks or böreks.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup freekeh
  • ¾ pound lamb meat, cut into 1 1/2-inch cubes
  • 1 onion, grated
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 bunch fresh cilantro, finely chopped, divided
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 bunch fresh mint, finely chopped, divided
  • Water as needed
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 3 medium ripe tomatoes

Steps

  1. Place freekeh in a small bowl and cover with cold water. Set aside.
  2. Combine lamb, onion, pepper, paprika, and salt in a pot. Add 1/2 of the mint, tomato paste, and oil; mix well. Simmer over low heat for 15 minutes. Pour in just enough water to cover, and return to a simmer.
  3. Set a steamer over the pot and add tomatoes. Cover and steam tomatoes until soft, about 5 minutes. Crush tomatoes using a wooden spoon, so pulp drips into soup. Remove the steamer and discard leftover tomato peels.
  4. Drain freekeh and add to soup. Simmer until soft, about 15 minutes. Remove celery stalk. Sprinkle soup with remaining cilantro and mint before serving.
